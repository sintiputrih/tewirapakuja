Aaron Carter, who is he?

Aaron Carter is an American rapper, singer-songwriter, actor, dancer, and record producer who rose to prominence in the late 1990s as a pop and hip hop singer.

Carter’s debut single, “Crush on You,” charted in Australia, Germany, and the United Kingdom in 1997. Aaron Carter, his debut album, was released in Europe in December 1997 and sold over one million copies worldwide. Aaron’s Party (Come Get It) (2000), Carter’s second album, sold three million copies in the United States. Carter has appeared in Lizzie McGuire, Sabrina the Teenage Witch, 7th Heaven, and Popstar as an actor.
