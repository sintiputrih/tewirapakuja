Emily Dunn Biography

Emily Dunn is an American actress and dancer who was born in Salt Lake City, Utah, who made her acting debut in Napoleon Dynamite in the year 2004, under her maiden name Emily Kennard.

The youngest of six children, she started dancing at age three, drawing at age 4, singing and acting at age 8. When she was 15, she started competing in International Standard and International Latin. At age 19, Emily earned a position on the Young Ambassadors, an international performing group for Brigham Young University (BYU).

She traveled across the United States and also to Brazil as well as an Argentina performing a show called Broadway Rhythm. She shot her feature film debut as Trisha in Napoleon Dynamite. At BYU, she studied acting classes with future co-star Jon Heder.
