Who is Jelly Roll?

Jason Bradley DeFord better known by his stage name Jelly Roll born December 4, 1984, is an American rapper and multi-genre singer and songwriter who in 2023, won three CMT Music Awards for the song “Son of a Sinner”.

At age 14, he and several others were arrested and charged with multiple counts of marijuana possession and attempted theft following an attempted marijuana theft.

The song is the second single from the 2021 album Ballads of the Broken and also peaked at number 8 on the Billboard Hot Rock & Alternative Songs chart.
