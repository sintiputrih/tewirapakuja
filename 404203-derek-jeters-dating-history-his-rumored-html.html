Derek Jeter's Dating History - His Rumored and Confirmed Girlfriends

Derek Jeter is a devoted husband and a loving father. But before he settled down, the baseball legend was romantically linked to high-profile women. Now fans want to know more about Derek Jeter's dating history.

Derek has been happily married to Hannah Davis since 2016. The couple shares three children, Story Grey, River Rose, and Bella Raine Jeter. The couple met in 2012 through mutual friends shortly after Derek had ended his relationship with actress Minka Kelly.

The pair began dating lowkey and engaged in 2015 before finally tying the knot in 2016. The lovebirds have since been focused on raising their family and kept much of their personal life private.

The father of three has lived in the spotlight since he began playing for the New York Yankees in 1995 aged 20. While fame opened many opportunities for the former baseball player, it also exposed his love life. Though now retired fans still want to know more about Derek Jeter's dating history.

Derek Jeter & Mariah Carey

Derek briefly dated music icon Mariah Carey in the late 90s when the two were one of the biggest stars of that time. Their relationship made headlines since the Grammy Award winner was still married to music executive Tommy Mottola.

During an interview, the singer revealed to Oprah Winfrey that her brief relationship with Derek was significant as it helped her walk away from her toxic relationship with Mottola.

The "We Belong Together" singer revealed to the talk show host that Derek was the "catalyst" for her divorce from Mottola, whom she termed controlling, admitting that she felt like a prisoner while living with him. Even so, their relationship was brief( from 1997 until 1998).

Derek Jeter & Lara Dutta

The former New York Yankees reportedly dated Dutta, an Indian model, and actress who won Miss Universe 2000 Pageant, for about a year. According to reports, Derek pursued the "Bell Bottom" actress after he saw her on an episode of "Live with Regis & Kathie Lee."

Though it's unclear when they began dating, they were spotted together at the New York Knicks-Vancouver Grizzlies game at Madison Square Garden in January 2000.

The two reportedly dated for a year and were publicly spotted together for the first time when they went clubbing to celebrate the baseball legend's 28th birthday in 2002. Soon after, the pair were spotted watching a Lakers game together.

Derek Jeter & Vanessa Lachey

The New York Yankees alumni reportedly dated actress, TV host, and former beauty queen Vanessa Lachey for almost three years. The two were in an on-and-off relationship from 2003 to 2006.

Nothing substantial came out of their relationship, and the two went their separate ways. Vanessa found love again and has been happily married to singer, actor, and TV host Nick Lachey since July 2011. The couple shares three children.

Derek was rumored to be dating "The Avengers" actress Scarlett Johanson in the early 2000s. Even though the two were reportedly seen leaving a Christmas party together, the award-winning actress denied the dating rumors.
