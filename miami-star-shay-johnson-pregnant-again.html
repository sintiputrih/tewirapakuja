MIAMI' STAR SHAY JOHNSON PREGNANT AGAIN?

 Posted on January 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  340 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Love & Hip Hop: Miami star Shay Johnson sent shockwaves through the reality TV world in the latest episode that aired on Monday night on VH1. The bombshell revelation? She is pregnant again.

In a candid moment with her mom Sandra, Shay declared, “I’m pregnant,” as she unveiled her baby bump.

The reality star’s announcement was met with disbelief. “That’s a shame. What the heck did you do that for?” her mom exclaimed, clearly taken aback by the unexpected news.

Shay, however, didn’t back down, stating, “I don’t even know what to say. I can tell you this, you helping me making my decision because i have been thinking about an abortion.”

“Now i know you ain’t doing that,” Shay’s mom retorted. “Why you get pregnant by him again?” 

To which Shay tearfully said, “What the hell you so mad for? I didn’t tell you to get this reaction.”

She continued, “Don’t get comfortable with this situation because it’s a situation that i do not want.”

Shay is already a mom to a one-year-old daughter, Shajiyah, whom she shares with her on and off boyfriend, Fabian(Fabo).

During the highly charged episode, Shay admitted to breaking up with her baby daddy Fabian, citing disrespect towards her mother as the breaking point.

However, it appears the couple may have reconciled as Shay posted a video just a day ago, showcasing her and Fabo on vacation..

“About last night! #Tipsy too much wine, #DominicanRepublic”, she captioned the clip.

The clip raises questions about the timeline, as a “tipsy” Shay is seen with a flat stomach, which has fans wondering if she went through with the abortion.

In a previous interview, Johnson hinted at her desire for another child. “I honestly think I want one more,” she told BET. “A boy! It honestly doesn’t matter, but in the perfect world, I would want a boy. I told Shajiyah’s father that I want one more. He said, ‘Let’s do it in about a year or two.’ I’m like, ‘No, I want one now.’”
