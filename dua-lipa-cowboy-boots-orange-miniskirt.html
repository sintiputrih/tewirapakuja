Dua Lipa Pairs Cowboy Boots with the Tiniest Orange Miniskirt

 Posted on February 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  319 words  |  Aldo Pusey

As the end of summer nears, Dua Lipa is leaning into fall colors through her sensational off-duty style.

In a photo dump posted to Instagram, the "Break My Heart" singer shared a look at a new outfit that provided a lesson in unique transitional dressing.

Her look was composed of a bright orange knit set with purple lining from Diesel's resort 2023 collection; it included a midriff-baring cardigan and miniskirt with a zip-up detail on the side that exposed her thigh. She wore the look with a classic pair of black Western cowboy boots.

Lipa has previously repped the beloved denim brand in other street style looks.

Most recently, the Grammy-winning singer was seen wearing Diesel's denim over-the-knee boots, featuring pointed toes and stiletto heels, while grabbing dinner with friends this past May. She paired the statement shoes with a long silk slipdress from Blumarine, which also included a denim print and a high leg slit, a slate blue bomber jacket, and a mix of silver and gold jewelry.

Prior to that, Lipa stepped out in New York City back in March while wearing a head-to-toe denim look composed of pieces from Glenn Martens's widely celebrated Diesel fall 2022 fashion show. The ensemble included a pair of the Italian brand's infamous pantaboots, this iteration featuring stilettos and a diamond-pattern print in acid wash. She paired the bottoms with a cropped zip-up denim bustier, again in acid wash, and carried a denim bag with a buckle strap. Accessories included a grungy belt bedazzled with Diesel's logo silver jewelry.
