Who is Lauren Conrad? 

Lauren Conrad is an American television personality best known for her roles on MTV’s Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County and its spin-off, The Hills. For this, she received the “Teen Choice Awards- Choice Female Reality TV.” Conrad is both a writer and a fashion designer.

Body Measurement

Lauren stands at 5 feet 5 inches tall and weighs 54 kilograms. Conrad’s hair is light brown, and her eyes are bright green.

Early Life

Lauren Conrad was born on February 1, 1986, as Lauren Katherine Conrad. Aquarius is her zodiac sign. She was born and raised in Laguna Beach, California. Kathy Conrad (father) and Jim Conrad (mother), both architects, are her parents. Breanna, her younger sister, and Brandon, her younger brother, are her siblings. Lauren is an American national who is of mixed ancestry, with English, German, and Irish ancestors.

Education

She went to Laguna Beach High School and then to the Academy of Art University in San Francisco for one semester of her study. She relocated to Los Angeles in 2006 to seek a career in the design industry and attended the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising.

Lifestyle

Lauren and her husband bought a 5-bedroom house in the Pacific Palisades neighborhood of Los Angeles for $4.4 million in 2015. They sold the residence in 2017 for just under $5 million. In 2016, she also sold a $2.8 million duplex condo in Beverly Hills.

Lauren paid $3.6 million for a property in Brentwood in 2013. She also owns two properties on the same street in Laguna Beach, where she grew up. She purchased two homes, one for $2 million in 2009 and the other for $8.5 million in 2014.

Married Life

Lauren Conrad is, in fact, a married woman. William Tell, her longtime boyfriend, is her husband. William, her husband, used to be a rhythm guitarist and backup vocalist. In the year 2012, however, they started dating. They married in California on September 13, 2014, after dating for a year.

Lauren revealed that the couple is expecting their first child on January 1, 2017. In addition, the couple has a son named Liam James Tell. They are currently residing in Brentwood, Los Angeles. Conrad has two canine companions named Chloe and Fitz.

Relationship Status

Lauren has previously dated fellow cast members Brody Jenner and Jason Wahler for short periods. Conrad was even linked to several sex tape rumors while working with Wahler. She was in a relationship with actor Kyle Howard in 2008. However, after three years of dating, the pair split up in 2011.

Shows on Television

Lauren Conrad began her television career in 2010 with the reality show ‘Kathy Griffin: My Life on the D-List.’ She published two sequels to her original work, ‘Sweet Little Lies’ and ‘Sugar and Spice,’ in the same year. She Style, her fashion book, was also released at the time.

Lauren, along with her hairstylist Kristin Ess and makeup artist Amy Nadine, founded The Beauty Department in April 2011. In August of that year, she debuted her third fashion collection, ‘Paper Crown.’ Conrad’s fourth novel, titled ‘The Fame Game,’ was published in April 2012. Lauren, in collaboration with BlueAvocado, debuted the XO series of eco-friendly bags in July (eco). She released a sequel to ‘The Fame Game,’ titled ‘Starstruck,’ in October 2012.

Similarly, the trilogy’s last novel, titled ‘Infamous,’ was published in 2013. Aside from that, in 2013, Conrad and her friend Hannah Skvarla launched the web store ‘The Little Market.’ In 2015, she launched a limited-edition Cinderella-inspired line in LC Lauren Conrad, followed by Minnie Mouse and Bambi-themed collections. As a way to develop her brand, Conrad launched a swim collection at Kohls in 2017.
