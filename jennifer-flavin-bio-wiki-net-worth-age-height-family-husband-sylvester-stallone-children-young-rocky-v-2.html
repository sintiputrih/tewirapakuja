Jennifer Flavin Biography and Wiki

Jennifer Flavin is an American actress, entrepreneur, business owner,   and former model She is best known for Rocky V (1990), The No Name Gang, and Bar Girls (1990). Flavin is widely known as the wife of actor and filmmaker Sylvester Stallone. Her way to prominence first began as a model at the age of 19 and made numerous appearances for many companies and brands.

Flavin has appeared in several documentary-style programs and has also done a few films and TV shows. She is currently,  the co-owner of beauty and a cosmetic company called Serious Skin Care. The company is known for selling its products via the Home Shopping Network. Moreover, she is quite known as a robust and independent woman.

Jennifer Flavin Husband, Married

Flavin met Sylvester Stallone in 1988, in California at a Beverly Hills restaurant. After dating for almost nine years, the two eventually got married on May 17, 1997, in a civil ceremony at The Dorchester Hotel in London, England, followed by a chapel ceremony at Blenheim Palace in Oxfordshire.

Jennifer’s husband Sylvester was born on July 6, 1946. He is the elder son of Francesco “Frank” Stallone Sr., a hairdresser and beautician, and Jacqueline “Jackie” Stallone, an astrologer, dancer, and promoter of women’s wrestling. He is an actor, director, screenwriter, and producer. Currently, She and Stallone reside in the Los Angeles neighborhood of Beverly Crest.

Jennifer Flavin Rocky V

Rocky is a 1976 American sports drama film directed by John G. Avildsen and both written by and starring Sylvester Stallone who is a husband to Jennifer. It is based on the rags to riches American Dream story of Rocky Balboa, an uneducated but kind-hearted working class Italian-American boxer working as a debt collector for a loan shark in the slums of Philadelphia.

Rocky, a small-time club fighter, gets a shot at the world heavyweight championship. The film also stars Talia Shire as Adrian, Burt Young as Adrian’s brother Paulie, Burgess Meredith as Rocky’s trainer Mickey Goldmill, and Carl Weathers as the champion, Apollo Creed.
