Bray Wyatt Parents: Meet Mike Rotunda And Stephanie Rotunda

 Posted on September 12, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  148 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Bray Wyatt was born to his parents Mike Rotunda and Stephanie Rotunda. His father Lawrence Michael Rotunda is also a well-known retired professional wrestler, however, not much is known regarding Stephanie Rotunda.

Stephanie Rotunda is the daughter of Robert Deroy Windham, better known by his ring name Blackjack Mulligan, who was also a professional wrestler, author, and American football player. Stephanie has two siblings, wrestlers Barry and Kendall Windham.

Bray Wyatt’s father, Mike Rotunda wrestled with the World Wrestling Federation and World Championship Wrestling in the 1980s and 1990s. Mike Rotunda has wrestled under the ring names Mike Rotunda, Mike Rotundo, Michael Wallstreet, Irwin R. Schyster, and V.K. Wallstreet.

Mike Rotunda and his wife, Stephanie Rotunda tied the knot in 1984. They have two sons and one daughter, Windham, Taylor, and Mika. Bray Wyatt is a three-time world champion in WWE who has held the WWE Championship once and the Universal Championship twice.
