How Behati Prinsloo, Adam Levine's Wife, Is Spending the Super Bowl

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  307 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Adam Levine and Maroon 5 might be the stars of the Super Bowl Halftime Show, but his wife, supermodel Behati Prinsloo, is also soaking up the big night. The Victoria's Secret Angel shared behind-the-scenes footage of the big game from her P.O.V. on social media, from backstage to the field.

The 30-year-old model started the sneak peeks early, kicking off with a shot of Levine and his band arriving to Atlanta's Mercedes-Benz Stadium. "Once in a life time....so proud of these boys @maroon5 #superbowl53 ðŸ’¥," she captioned a photo of the artists walking onto the field.

Then tonight, ahead of Levine's Halftime Show performance, Prinsloo gave us a look at Levine's pre-show process. Apparently, according to a Boomerang on her Instagram Story, the pampering routine includes charcoal eye masks.

"Yassssssss I'm more nervous," Behati captioned the clip of her hubby in a white cutoff tee, printed pants, and black sneakers.

An attendee caught the couple making their way through the stadium.

Prinsloo also shared a selfie video with her girls backstage, showing off her pass. Clearly, she was excited.

She also shared a clip of her sitting in the bleachers. The model's game day style includes a puffer jacket, a high ponytail in a scrunchie, hoop earrings, jeweled choker, and a black tee.

She was clearly pumped in selfies with friends from the stands.

Prinsloo and Levine married in 2014 and have two young daughters together: Dusty Rose Levine, who was born in September 2016, and Geo Grace Levine, who was born in February 2018. Unfortunately, it appears their little girls didn't join them at the game.
