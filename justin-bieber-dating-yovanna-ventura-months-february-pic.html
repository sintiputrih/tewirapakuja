Justin Bieber & Yovanna Ventura:  Dating For Months

Our source tells us that Justin messaged Yovanna on Instagram in February 2014. At the time she was in her senior year at Miami Senior High School, and Justin invited her to hang out with him at Miami’s Hit Factory recording studio. It turns out that this was their first date, and they’ve been dating ever since. But why did we just find out about the couple recently? It turns out Yovanna is really “shy” and didn’t want anyone to know about dating the Biebs.

“Yes, they are dating. She’s kept it to herself,” our source says. “When people found out [about Bieber] she acted like nothing happened but she was telling her friends he was a really chill, cute, honest, and sweet guy.

It turns out that Justin is telling Yovanna that he wants to help her with her modeling career.

“As a model she is using the [Bieber] brand as a way to help her,” our source adds. “He is trying to help bring her up the ranks.”

But it wasn’t very hard for Yovanna to fall for Justin because it turns out she’s had a crush on him for years!

“She used to have pics of Justin on her phone and posters of him in her room growing up,” the source tells us. “She has always liked him.”

But this is where things get really sticky because we thought that Justin and Selena were back together? Just recently on June 11 they were together and posting romantic pictures on Instagram. Then they were spotted on June 16 at Alfredo Flores‘ birthday party.

But then we learned at HollywoodLife.com that they had a falling out before the Fourth of July weekend, and that’s when he ran back to Yovanna.
