The video of a heavily pregnant woman doing some amazing dance moves on a pole is currently taking the internet by storm.

Thirty-five-year-old Allison Sipes, hailing from Florida, was not ready to give up her passion for pole dancing when she learned that she was going to become a mom. Her growing belly did not stop her from hitting the dance floor. Allison is now 9 months pregnant and is due to give birth in a few days time.

#37weeks pregnant doing some #pregnantyoga and a #pdironx check in! Not long now before baby comes! #lotusfitnessstudio

A post shared by Allison Sipes (@alliegatoruf) on Mar 22, 2018 at 9:29am PDT

The pole-dancing video of 37 weeks pregnant Allison is shared by hundreds of people on the social media and many say that Allison's videos are inspiring for others as well.

#36weekspregnant Spinning after class yesterday at @lotusfitnessstudio wearing my @onzie shorts! Seeing what I can still manage on the pole and having fun in the air.

A post shared by Allison Sipes (@alliegatoruf) on Mar 15, 2018 at 5:10am PDT

In many videos, Allison’s belly too could be seen moving and the viewers are excited to watch the movements of the unborn child who clearly enjoys mommy’s dancing sessions. Allison has been doing pole dancing since she was 13 years old. She says that with regular consultation with the doctor and expert training, anyone can continue doing physical activities even during pregnancy. However, the soon-to-be mother added that determination is the most important factor in maintaining a healthy body and mind.
