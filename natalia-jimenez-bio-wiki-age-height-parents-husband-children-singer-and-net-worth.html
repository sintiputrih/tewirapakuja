Natalia Jiménez Biography

Natalia Jiménez Born Natalia Altea Jimenez Sarmento is a Spanish singer-songwriter born in Madrid, Spain. She began her music career at 15 singing on streets and subways of Madrid and by 2000 she became a vocalist lead singer of La Quinta Estacion a deal agreement she signed with Sony Music.

She has Awards from Grammy and Latin Grammy both in her solo and duets, among duets she has featured stars of Latin Music including Daddy Yankee, Marc Anthony, and Ricky Martin.
