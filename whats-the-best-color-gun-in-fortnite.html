The best color gun in Fortnite is the gold Mythic gun. It is the most powerful and accurate weapon in the game.

What Colour are the best guns in Fortnite?

The best guns in Fortnite are color-coded as follows:

  • Gray: Common.
  • Green: Uncommon.
  • Blue: Rare.
  • Purple: Epic.
  • Orange: Legendary.
  • Gold: Mythic.

Is purple or blue better in Fortnite?

Blue weapons are slightly better than purple weapons in terms of damage. Purple or Epic weapons are the second-best rarity in the game.

What is the best gun in Fortnite right now?

The MK-Alpha Assault Rifle is currently considered the best gun in Fortnite. It has high stability, low recoil, and unmatched accuracy, making it the best choice for long-range combat.

What color is the rarest weapon in Fortnite?

In Fortnite, the rarest colored weapon is gold. Gold and purple weapons are the most sought-after as they are the most powerful and accurate.

Rank of Weapon Colors in Fortnite Battle Royale: from most damage to least powerful

There is no specific ranking of weapon colors in terms of damage. The power of weapons is determined by the color rarity, with gold being the most powerful and gray being the weakest.

Is Mythic better than Legendary in Fortnite?

There is technically no difference between Mythic and Legendary weapons in Fortnite. Mythic weapons are considered a subset of Legendary weapons.
