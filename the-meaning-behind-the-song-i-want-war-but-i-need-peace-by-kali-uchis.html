The Struggle Between Conflict and Harmony

At its core, “i want war (BUT I NEED PEACE)” explores the internal struggle between yearning for confrontation and the desire for inner tranquility. Kali Uchis uses the metaphorical language to express the paradoxical nature of our emotions, exposing the deeply rooted conflicts that exist within us. The song portrays the artist’s raw vulnerability as she navigates through the complexities of her own desires and seeks emotional equilibrium.

The lyrics reveal a yearning for conflict and revenge (“I want war, I’m not afraid”) juxtaposed with a profound longing for peace and emotional healing (“I need peace, give it to me”). This internal contradiction mirrors the inner battles many individuals face when trying to reconcile their inner demons with their desire for serenity. The song identifies the human tendency to desire conflict as a means of finding closure or seeking justice, while still acknowledging the necessity of peace for true contentment and growth.
