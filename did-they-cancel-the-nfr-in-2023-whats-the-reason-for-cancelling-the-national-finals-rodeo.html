Did they Cancel the NFR in 2023? What's the Reason for Cancelling the National Finals Rodeo? - Sarka

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  493 words  |  Martina Birk

News: The Wrangler National Finals Rodeo (NFR) slated for 2023 has been called off. The decision came from the Las Vegas Events Board of Trustees and the Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association (PRCA) Board of Directors. This unfortunate cancellation follows a devastating shooting incident at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas (UNLV) on Wednesday, resulting in the loss of three lives and leaving one individual critically wounded, including the assailant.

In a gesture of respect for the lives lost in this tragic event, the first performance of the NFR scheduled for Thursday, December 7, has been canceled. Las Vegas Events and the PRCA extend their heartfelt condolences. More information regarding the future of the NFR will be announced on Thursday morning.

Even though the NFR performance has been canceled, the Cowboy Channel Cowboy Christmas event at the Las Vegas Convention Center will continue as scheduled. The event, spanning the South and Central Halls, remains open to the public from Thursday, December 7, through Saturday, December 16.

The event organizers extend their sincere gratitude to the first responders, police, security personnel, and the UNLV community for their rapid and courageous response during the tragic incident. Their thoughts and prayers stand with all those affected by this senseless act of violence on the UNLV campus.

Reasons behind the cancellation of the National Finals Rodeo for 2023

Respecting the lives lost in the tragic shooting at the UNLV campus on Wednesday, the decision to cancel Thursday’s scheduled performance was made. The incident resulted in the loss of three lives and severe injuries to one person, including the assailant.

Las Vegas Events and the Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association (PRCA) conveyed profound concern and sorrow in light of this distressing incident. Their primary concern remains with the victims and their families during this challenging period. The decision to cancel the NFR was a direct response to this senseless act of violence, with further information about the event’s future expected to be disclosed on Thursday morning.

In summary, the 2023 Wrangler National Finals Rodeo (NFR) stands canceled following the tragic shooting incident at the UNLV campus. The cancellation of the initial performance was a tribute to the lives lost and impacted by this unfortunate event. The organizers extend their heartfelt condolences and prioritize supporting the victims and their families. Further information about the NFR’s future plans is anticipated to be revealed shortly.
