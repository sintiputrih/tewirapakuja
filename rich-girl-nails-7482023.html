8 Ways to Wear Rich-Girl Nails, According to an Expert

 Posted on March 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  570 words  |  Larita Shotwell

With TikTok fawning over old-money aesthetics, the vibes were bound to reach our nail-art mood boards eventually. So if you're looking to get a manicure that perfectly encapsulates quiet luxury, then rich-girl nails are about to become your new go-to look.

Coined by celebrity manicurist Tom Bachik (whose A-list clientele includes Jennifer Lopez and Selena Gomez), the term rich-girl nails described "perfectly manicured, perfectly polished, medium-long to long, full-coverage nude nails," he tells InStyle. "Fresh, clean, and understated, but deliberate, is the vibe." But the best part of this ultra-chic look is just how easy it is to do on your own...no on-call glam squad needed.

How Do You Get Rich-Girl Nails?

Rich-girl nails start with how you shape and file your nail, Bachik says. The classic rich-girl nail is long and filed in a tapered square shape, which he says helps elongate your fingers. Next is the color choice: Stick with a full-coverage neutral shade that is either one shade darker or lighter than your own skin tone. Then, finish off with a glossy topcoat for some shine and you're good to go.

It seems straightforward enough. But, as with any trend, there are countless ways to put your own spin on it.
