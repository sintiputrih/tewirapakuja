Watch Donny Osmond Sing to Betty White in This Heartwarming Throwback Video

 Posted on October 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  486 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Donny Osmond went on a trip down memory lane, where he stumbled upon a video of himself singing to Betty White. The singer proudly shared the video with his followers, tagging the legendary actress in the process.

Donny Osmond shared a lovely video of himself serenading Betty White on Instagram, much to their fans' delight. The singer couldn't help but reminisce about the good time they had before wishing the 99-year-old singer a great day.

In the video, both Osmond and White are standing up as he held both her hands with his right hand. His left grasped a microphone as he sang lovingly while looking into her eyes.

THEIR OWN LITTLE WORLD

White looked delighted as she swayed along with the singer before briefly putting her hands on his shoulders. Osmond interjected his singing by asking someone near them to take photos because he wanted to keep the memory alive.

The two icons looked completely in sync as they stared at each other, dancing as if there was no one else around them. People could be seen documenting the heartwarming moment behind them, but they paid no attention to them.

People flooded the video's comment section with praises, saying that it was indeed a priceless moment. Some fans were even brought to tears by the touching moment.

LEGENDS IN THEIR FIELDS

Osmond and White have made names for themselves in the entertainment industry. In fact, some people might even argue that they are one of the best in their fields.

At 99 years old, White has gone through all the ups and downs of life. Most recently, she worked on getting her favorite project, "The Pet Set," re-released. Despite the project, she stays home most of the time to protect herself from the Covid-19 pandemic.

ADVICE TO YOUNGER SELF

After all the success he's gained through the years, Osmond once shared what he would like to tell his younger self if he had the chance. His advice was that "Little Donny" should stay positive and "keep dreaming big."

If there's anyone who could know where staying positive and dreaming big would take Little Donny, it would be Osmond himself. He has achieved so much in the past five decades, and he's grown to be one of the most well-loved singers of all time.

DONNY'S GOOD LIFE

The singer first rose to fame in the 1970s when he and his older brothers were in The Osmonds. Later on, he and his sister Marie had an '80s variety series titled "Donny & Marie." The duo had an 11-year residency in Las Vegas in more recent years.

Marie and Donnie Osmond performing on stage | Source: Getty Images

Osmond came from a large brood. When he married his wife Debbie, they went on to have five sons named Donald Jr., Jeremy, Brandon, Christopher, and Joshua. All five are married, and the singer is blessed with 12 grandchildren.
