Bahati Bukuku Biography

Bahati Bukuku (Bahati Lusako Bukuku) is a Tanzanian gospel musician who is popularly known for her hit song ‘Dunia Haina Huruma’.

Bahati Bukuku  Age

The Tanzanian gospel singer has never opened up about her age or her date of birth.

Bahati Bukuku Parents

She was born as Bahati Lusako Bukuku to John and Enea Bukuku. Her father died in November 2013 following a short illness while her mother died on 12th January 2016 after she succumbed to complications arising from high blood pressure.

Bahati Bukuku Death Rumors

In 2014, Bukuku was involved in a bad road accident. She injured her back and was admitted to Muhimbili hospital in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

Bahati Bukuku Husband

Bahati was married to Daniel Basila but they later divorced and she came out to set the record straight after speculations that her husband divorced her after she was caught cheating with a doctor who was attending to her husband so that the husband could be discharged since they couldn’t raise the cash.

This speculation arose when she released her song ‘Dunia Haina Huruma’ which chronicles the event. She said that she faced some of the problems and issues portrayed in the video.

‘My now ex-husband did not do such a thing. Though there’s a part of the song which was drawn from my real-life experience. Irreconcilable differences were the premise for our divorce. I would lie about a man who was my husband, even if I author a book, I will always say I was married before but the union did not work out.’

Bahati Bukuku Allows  Ex-Husband To Re-mary

The Tanzanian gospel songbird Bahati Bukuku has spoken for the first time about her divorce.

The Dunia Haina Huruma hitmaker set the record straight and gave her estranged ex-husband of 14 years, Daniel Basila, the green light to re-marry.

However, according to sources, the 14 years period was rocky and the two were said to be living separately despite being married in a church, where they met.

It is alleged that Basila got tired of the kind of life he was living with Bukuku and decided to move from their matrimonial home to the countryside where he reportedly found his new love.

Sources reveal that Basila met Ngaiza, who is also a gospel singer in Dodoma, Tanzania, and she initially had no knowledge that he was Bukuku’s ex-husband.

During the time, they were blessed to have a kid who later died.
