Jaden “Jaymo” Rice On Netlix’s Cheer Season 2

Jaden “Jaymo” Rice is a member of the rival team Trinity Valley Community College who is ready to challenge their rival team and last year’s champion Navarro College. Jaymo tumbles alongside his star sister Angel Rice at TVCC. He has grown close with Johnson and Ben his two teammates.

He is appearing alongside his co-stars Jada Wooten, a second-year tumbler and flyer, Jeron Hazelwood, TVCC’s star stunter and tumbler, Devonte ‘Dee’ Joseph, one of TVCC’s new tumblers who can successfully pull off a “quint” that is so difficult it’s illegal to perform at Daytona, Angel Rice, a Guinness Book of World Records holder for most double full twists and a former member of Team USA gymnastics, and Ben “Benji” Chester who is known for his flexibility. Jaden “Jaymo” Rice Career

Jaden had previously cheered for the Stingray Allstars. In May 2018, he had announced making it on the Stingray All-Stars team with an IG post, “Your boy just made Steel again #steelrays#imaderays“.
