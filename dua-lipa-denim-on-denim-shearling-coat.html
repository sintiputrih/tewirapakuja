Dua Lipa Proves She Can Pull Off Anything with This Outfit Change

 Posted on September 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  318 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Dua Lipa's latest Instagram photo dump involves two major looks that prove her sartorial versatility.

In the first outfit, the "Don't Stop Now" singer wore denim on denim, courtesy of two pieces pulled from Givenchy's spring 2023 collection. The baggy denim jacket and denim midi skirt featured a faded grunge wash and the Givenchy logo printed all over. Topping the look off, Lipa opted for Givenchy's knee-high Shark boots in black leather.

In other photos, Lipa changed into a more feminine outfit composed of a midi dress emblazoned with a bold green-and-red plaid pattern and trimmed with frilly white lace on the sleeves and hem. She layered the dress with an oversized leather jacket by Prada with caramel brown shearling lining. She coordinated with knee-high black rubber boots and two black barrettes pinning back her hair.

The Grammy-winning singer is known for making a splash on Instagram by sharing a peek at some of her enviable street style looks.

Earlier this month, Lipa showed off a preppy look, wearing a navy blue Gucci sweater vest emblazoned with the house's signature GG motif in a vibrant red jacquard pattern with a white striped collared shirt, black pinstripe blazer, and crimson red iteration of Gucci's Jackie 1961 purse.

And back in December, she posted photos of an edgier look, which included a black coat and matching micro miniskirt in a metallic vinyl finish, plus a slate gray top, sheer black tights, and a pair of knee-high patent-leather boots with curved stiletto heels.
