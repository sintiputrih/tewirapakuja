Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jackie Jackson’s journey to fame began in Gary, Indiana, where he was born as the second child of the Jackson family. His early exposure to music, alongside his brothers, led to the formation of the Jackson 5. This group would go on to become one of the most celebrated acts in music history, laying the foundation for Jackie’s financial success.

The Jackson 5 Era

The Jackson 5, under the Motown label, achieved remarkable success with hits like “I Want You Back” and “ABC.” Jackie’s role in the group not only showcased his musical talents but also began to build his personal wealth.
