Stefon Mar’sean Diggs is an American football wide receiver for the Buffalo Bills of the National Football League (NFL)

Where did Stefon Diggs go to college? Did Stefon Diggs play college football?

Stefon Diggs attended Our Lady of Good Counsel High School in where he played football and ran track. He was a first-team All-metro selection at wide receiver by The Washington Post and All-county selection by the Montgomery Gazette.

After his senior season, he was named MVP of the U.S. Army All-American Junior Combine in 2011. In addition, he was invited to play in the 2012 U.S. Army All-American Bowl.

After high school, he committed to the University of Maryland over scholarship offers from Florida, USC, Cal, Ohio State, and Auburn, among others. He played on coach Randy Edsall’s Maryland Terrapins football team from 2012 to 2014.

After his junior season, he decided to forgo his senior season and entered the 2015 NFL Draft.
