Plies | Career

Plies released his debut album, “The Real Testament,” in August 2007.

T-Pain was featured on the album’s lead song, “Shawty,” which peaked at number one on the Billboard Hot Rap Tracks chart.

The RIAA certified the album gold within six months, and the next two singles experienced comparable success.

Only ten months after his debut record was released, his second album, “Definition of Real,” was made available in June 2008.

“Bust It Baby Pt. 2,” the album’s lead single featuring Ne-Yo, reached its highest point at number two on the Hot Rap Tracks and seven on the Hot 100.

The album sold over 200,000 copies in its first week and peaked at number two on the Billboard 200.

Four months after its debut, the album received its gold certification.

He released his third album, “Da REAList,” in December 2008. In its first week, the studio album sold more than 110,000 copies.
